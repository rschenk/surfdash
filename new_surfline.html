Central Florida PM Report: 
Strong E windswell in the water this morning with plenty of chest-head high surf for most of the region and a few larger overhead sets at standouts. Conditions remain bumpy/choppy and disorganized with moderate to breezy onshore ESE/SE winds. Not much quality surf to track down other than select spots with some wind protection. Low tide is at 1:30pm. 


Short Term Forecast:  
Look for plenty of head high+ windswell to maintain through the day. Onshore ESE trending SE winds remain moderate to breezy which keeps some bump/chop on it but could be worse. Key spots with some southerly wind protection may offer better conditions.
